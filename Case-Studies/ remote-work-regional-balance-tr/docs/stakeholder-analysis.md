## Stakeholder Analysis

### Primary Stakeholders
- Remote employees / job seekers
- Employers hiring remote roles
- Central government bodies
- Municipalities in pilot cities

### Stakeholder Needs
- Employees: stability, career path, reliable workspace/internet
- Employers: performance, security, predictable legal framework, incentives
- Government: measurable impact, fiscal feasibility, social cohesion
- Municipalities: local economic activation, youth retention
