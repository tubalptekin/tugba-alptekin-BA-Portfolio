# ✨ EPIC — Flight Disruption Management

**As an airline operations organization**,
we need to manage flight delays efficiently by coordinating OCC decisions, airport operations, passenger communication, and system updates,
so that operational impact is minimized and passengers receive timely, clear information.

## Features Included in This Epic
#### 1. Passenger Notification & Communication

- Sending SMS, in-app, and email updates

- Displaying delay reason & revised ETD

- Allowing passengers to view rebooking options

#### 2. Operational Decision Management (OCC)

- Evaluating delay reason and duration

- Publishing new ETD/ETA events

- Triggering downstream operational flows

#### 3. Gate Assignment Management

- Detecting gate conflicts

- Updating gate allocation

- Syncing FIDS, airport staff, and ground services

#### 4. Baggage Handling Adjustment

- Re-routing bags

- Syncing BHS/BRS based on new timelines

- Preventing misrouting or transfer bag loss

#### 5. Crew & Aircraft Resource Recalculation

- Crew legality check

- Aircraft rotation impact

- Ensuring continuity for next flight legs
